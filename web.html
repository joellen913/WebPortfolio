websites from my internship at Management Solutions Plus


From May 2022 - Auguat 2022 I had an internship in Rockville, Maryland with MSP as a fullstack web designer, where I redesigned websites for a multitude of their clients

↓

IFTA
For this client, I was tasked to develop a site to showcase their range of technical analysts from around the world, as well as update their monthly blogs for the IFTA conventions, add images from previous conventions, and more.
ATAP
I was tasked in creating the background graphics for the home page of this site, as well as to add a pop up promoting their annual conference, as well as their Talent Acquisition week occurring in 2023. I also updated the blog each week during my internship, added their social medias, created other pages with various forms of content, and much more.
NRHC
For this site, I was tasked to use PHP to create pop ups for each state on the Single family rental property page, as well as adding in the graphics and text for the select pages.
FJA
I developed this site for my internship to inform users about the federal judges association, and how the company assists clients. I added images and text to various pages, as well as used PHP to create the membership page, and added mockup images from photoshop to several of the pages.
ACMA
I was tasked to create the ACMA site in order to promote their annual conference in Utah of 2022. I created the background graphic of the opening page in adobe illustrator and Photoshop, as well as adding in all of the information, and using several different tags to create a sponsor page with the appropriate sponsor logos.
NAACO
this site was created in order to promote NAACO events. I had to create seven different pages advertising their membership, as well as the annual conferences. I use several different titles to create a grid containing all of the sponsors as well as adding in an annual conference 2023 Graphic, taking place in Vancouver.
IWIL
For this site, I was tasked with adding all of the graphics to each page, as well as adding in the text. I created the different subpages using PHP, as well as pop ups for the Well-Being Week schedule.
FBA
For the federal Bar Association, I had the job of creating this site which contained news and events regarding federal lawyers. I use PHP to update the member to his page, as well as many reference links for the Government's Relations page.
Shock
For this client, I was tasked to develop a site to showcase their range of technical analysts from around the world, as well as update their monthly blogs for the IFTA conventions, add images from previous conventions, and more.
